# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Magic: The Gathering players using Drawspell as a shared remote tabletop, often while actively playing and managing cards across desktop and mobile devices. They need to read game state and move cards quickly without the interface competing with the battlefield.

## Product Purpose

Drawspell provides a fast, realtime shared tabletop for playing Magic: The Gathering remotely. Success means players can understand the board, manage cards and player state, and complete common game actions with minimal interruption or explanation.

## Brand Personality

Minimal, fast, mobile-friendly. The interface should feel focused, responsive, and familiar to players without imitating a physical tabletop at the expense of usability.

## Anti-references

Avoid dense control panels, decorative tabletop chrome, and important interactions available only through hover. Avoid unfamiliar controls that require players to learn interface-specific conventions before they can play.

## Design Principles

- Keep the battlefield and cards visually primary.
- Make common game actions direct, visible, and quick.
- Use familiar zone and control conventions consistently across screen sizes.
- Preserve readable game state while progressively disclosing secondary controls.
- Adapt the composition structurally for mobile instead of merely shrinking desktop UI.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast and interaction requirements. Support keyboard navigation, reduced motion, touch input, and state cues that do not depend on color alone. Do not hide essential actions behind hover-only interactions.
